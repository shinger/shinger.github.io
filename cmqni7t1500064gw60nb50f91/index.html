On dreams - Arrival or not

笙歌/二零二六年 三月 二十七日/约 255 字/阅读约 1 分钟

Last night I got an anxious dream. I dreamed I ended my school and went home. I don’t konw how did I got my home but it’s all by myself alone. For a while at home, I suddenly remembered that I always took a shool bus to home. And I realized there’s not too much time for me, because school bus will arrive in few minutes and they only wait there for 10-20 minutes. So I rushed out. And I think I was running along the shortcut but it would still take a long time for there. I opened my taxi app, but it just too exhausted to make a right route. I spent a long time but couldn’t do it while I was still running. Anxious, nervous, all in this dream. And then I woke up. But it was that time, I suddenly found that there is no need to go back and take the school bus since I have arrived at home. Because arrival at home is my destination, and I have done it, so there is no need to take a bus again.

After that, I noticed that this dream is actually a delusion of the reverse of the logic. And I think a lot for it, because it’s not just a dream, it’s my unconsciousness. In my real life, there might be another reverse. Maybe I have ‘got home’, but I still too anxcious to take back to try to take a ‘school bus’.
